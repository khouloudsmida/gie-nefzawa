About us

Nefzawa groupement for media developement and production «GIE-NEF»

The most appropriate legal form for setting up a financing mechanism capable of guaranteeing the sustainability of community media . The GIE-NEF is governed by Articles 439 to 460 of the Commercial Companies Code . It is a form of commercial society made up of members of nefzawa along with an organization and a women's center, and it is endowed with legal identity ()to promote economic activity and enhance or boost the outcomes of that activity.
